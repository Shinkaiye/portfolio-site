About me

Pulling inspiration from the do-it-yourself aesthetic of the early punk scene, and having a strong understanding of the fundamentals of design, Tobi Shinkaiye can assemble contrasting elements in a harmonious way. Born and raised in Lagos, Nigeria and currently living in Hamilton, they bring a unique perspective to each design. With an interest in print and brand design, Shinkaiye will provide dynamic and engaging designs.
